Covid-19 was first discovered last December 31, 2019. It has since spread to many other countries, causing a pandemic. Older people and those who have underlying medical conditions are most susceptible to this disease and are more prone to getting severe cases.

To help reduce the spread of this virus, people should follow the safety precautions that official health workers recommend. These include wearing a mask and face shield, especially when going outdoors. People should stay within a safe distance around others since the virus primarily spreads through water droplets in the air.

A more permanent solution to ending this pandemic would be vaccination. Covid vaccines help lessen a person's chances of getting more severe cases if/when infected. There is also proof that vaccines could help reduce the frequency of viral mutations, making an area less likely to experience future outbreaks.
