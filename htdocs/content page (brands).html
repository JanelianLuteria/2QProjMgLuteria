Moderna

dosage:

2 shots, 28 days apart

type of vaccine:

mRNA

who should get this vaccine?

The Moderna vaccine is recommended for people ages 18 years and older.

If you have had a severe allergic reactions to any ingredient inside an mRNA COVID-19 vaccine

Johnson & Johnson's Janssen

dosage:

1 shot may be given in some situations.

type of vaccine:

viral vector

who should get this vaccine?

Those who have had a severe reaction after an mRNA vaccine dose or who have a severe allergy to an ingredient of Pfizer-BioNTech or Moderna, Would otherwise remain unvaccinated for COVID-19 due to limited access to Pfizer-BioNTech or Moderna (mRNA COVID-19 vaccines), Want to get the J&J/Janssen COVID-19 vaccine despite the safety concerns

who shouldn't get this vaccine?

If you have had a severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis) or an immediate allergic reaction, even if it was not severe, to any ingredient
